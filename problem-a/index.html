Mindy Lahiri

Mindy Lahiri is the main character of the TV show The Mindy Project. She is a 30-ish-year-old gynecologist in New York City. She is quirky, funny, and real, and gives us a glimpse into her exciting but turbulent professional and personal life.

She is awesome because:

  • She is not afraid to be who she is
  • She has ups and downs just like anyone else
  • She is funny and takes things in stride
